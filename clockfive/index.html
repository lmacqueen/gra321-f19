1% of the population identify as asexual or aspec, meaning they experience little to no sexual attraction. The few media representations of asexuality are mired in problematic tropes.

Alien

Representing asexuality as alien or otherwise inhuman otherizes and marginalizes asexuals

Pathological

Associating asexuality with serial killers and psychopaths makes the identity seem like a sickness.

Repulsive

Coding “undesirable” groups with asexuality furthers the misconceptions that asexuals identify that way because they can’t get a date.

Fixable

Portraying asexuality being “fixed” by falling in love conveys the idea that asexuals don’t understand themselves.

Traumatized

Showing asexuality being formed as a result of trauma portrays the identity as a mental illness.

Prude

Conflating asexuality (an identity) with celibacy (a choice) sets problematic expectations about sex in asexual relationships.

Immature

Showing asexuality as a phase that people grow out of invalidates people who have identified as asexual for their entire lives.
